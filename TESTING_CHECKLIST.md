# Testing Checklist

## Playability

□ Can a new player understand what to do?

□ Can the player interact?

□ Does the game respond?

## Feedback

□ Does the game tell players when they are correct?

□ Are rewards clear?

□ Are mistakes handled positively?

## Kindergarten Friendly

□ Large buttons

□ Simple instructions

□ Clear visuals

□ Not too much information

## Technical

□ Game starts successfully

□ No major errors

□ Works after refreshing

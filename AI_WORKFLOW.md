# Working with AI

AI is your teammate. It can help you understand code and build your game one small step at a time.

Do not only ask: “Make me a game.” Explain what you are building, who the user is, what the player does, and what problem you have.

For example:

> I am creating a game for kindergarten children.
>
> The player needs to drag animals into the correct habitat.
>
> Help me:
> 1. design the game logic;
> 2. explain which files need changes;
> 3. implement one feature at a time.

## A simple workflow

1. Ask AI to explain.
2. Ask AI to plan.
3. Make one change.
4. Test.
5. Commit if it works.

If something breaks, tell AI what you expected, what happened instead, and include any error message. Test the game after every small change.
